Jordy van Langen

I am Jordy van Langen, a recent graduate from the research master ‘Behavioural Science’ at Radboud University Nijmegen (2017-2019). At the Donders Centre for Cognitive Neuroimaging, I completed my master's research, studyying cognitive load and anxiety in Parkinon’s tremor (PI's: Rick Helmich & Karin Roelofs). Currently, I work as a research assisstant at the Brain and Mind Centre, University of Sydney, Australia (PI: Simon Lewis in collaboration with Rick Helmich). If you would like to ask me about my research, you can always e-mail me on: jordy.vanlangen@sydney.edu.au or have a look at my Twitter: @jordyvanlangen.
